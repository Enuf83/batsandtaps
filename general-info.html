About Bats & Taps

Established in 2026, Bats and Taps brings a fresh take on sports training and recreation to the SE Portland metro. Bats and Taps (BAT) primarily operates as a batting cage but offers sports themed games and activities for everyone to enjoy.

The large space is designed to promote an ideal experience for the athlete in training as well as the game loving kid in all of us.

There are arcades, yard games, TVs, food, beer and more, all in addition to 4 full length and fully equipped batting cages.

The atmosphere is bright and cheerful, and the vibe is lively and upbeat.

Love/passion for baseball and softball.

BAT strives to be a place that the whole family can enjoy, and a place that the community can be proud of, for the peace, fun and refreshment it brings.

You can watch a game while enjoying food and beer from our ballpark inspired menu.
