Meet Our Team

Our passionate crew brings the energy, fun, and love of the game to every visitor at Bats and Taps!

Mike Thompson

Owner & Founder

Mike is the heart and soul behind Bats and Taps. A lifelong baseball enthusiast, he dreamed up this vibrant space to bring families, athletes, and game lovers together. When he's not swinging a bat or pouring a craft beer, he's planning the next big event!

Sarah Rivera

Chief Operating Officer

Sarah keeps everything running smoothly with her 80s energy and organizational superpowers. From scheduling cages to managing our neon-lit arcade, she makes sure every day at Bats and Taps is a home run.

Alex Chen

General Manager

Alex is the on-the-ground leader who greets every guest with a smile. A former college softball player, she loves coaching our youth workshops and making sure the taps are flowing and the games are on!

Jordan Lee

Lead Associate & Events Coordinator

Jordan is our go-to person for epic events and customer fun. With a passion for retro games and softball trivia, Jordan keeps the vibe lively and the arcade humming all day long.
